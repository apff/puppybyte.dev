About Xpenses

Xpenses was developed with love by a couple who was looking for a way to take control of their finances and budgets in a private and subscription-free way, with all the intuitive visual reports they needed.

"We wanted an app that put privacy first, let us share expenses with family members easily, and could be customized to fit the way we live.

We made this app for ourselves, and we decided to share it because we believe it can help other families and individuals too. In the end, Xpenses is more than just an app—it's a reflection of our values."

The PuppyByte Team
